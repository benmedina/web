How to move the pieces?

The pawns can move two spaces up on their first move and one space up every move after that. They can capture one square diagonally forward. Pawns are worth 1 "point".
Rooks can move and capture along any file, but not diagonally. Rooks are worth 5 "points."
Knights can move and capture in an L shape, going two squares up, down, left, or right and then moving one square to the left or right. Knights are worth 3 "points"
Bishops can move and capture along any diagonal, but can only stay on their color (ex: white's black squared bishops can only move along black squared diagonals). Bishops are worth 3 "points".
Queens can move and capture along any file or diagonal, as many squares as you want. Queens are worth 8 "points".
Kings can move and capture one square in an direction.

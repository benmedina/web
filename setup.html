How to setup the board?

The white pieces go on the first and second ranks, while the black pieces go on the seventh and eight ranks. The white pawns go along the second rank and the black pawns go along the seventh rank.
Here's what this should look like.
Starting from A1, the white pieces are ordered as follows: Rook, Knight, Bishop, Queen, King, Bishop, Knight, Rook Starting from A8, the black pieces are ordered in the same way.
